## About

I am a data analyst by role and a data scientist by practice.

My work focuses on building end-to-end systems that take messy real-world data and turn it into structured, usable outputs. I am particularly interested in problems that require combining data engineering, machine learning, and product thinking.

Most of what I build follows a simple pattern:

ingest → structure → reason → ship

## Projects

### Municipalities Budget Scraper
AI-powered ETL pipeline ingesting and structuring budget data across 3,696 Canadian municipalities.

- Designed for nationwide coverage  
- Converts unstructured documents into structured datasets  
- Built for @BuildCanada  

[Repo](https://github.com/Andrew-Girgis/Municipalities-Budget-Scraper)

---

### Computare
Privacy-first personal finance platform.

- Universal transaction schema for cross-source normalization  
- AI-driven categorization and forecasting  
- Deterministic subscription detection  
- Designed for local-first and user-owned data  

[Repo](https://github.com/Andrew-Girgis/Computare)
